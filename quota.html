Fair Usage Guidelines

Help our supplies last longer with these considerate usage tips

Do's

  • Use reasonable portions of coffee beans
  • Only take what you need for your drink
  • Share the coffee corner with colleagues
  • Clean up after yourself
  • Report when supplies are running low
  • Be mindful during busy periods

Don'ts

  • Don't take supplies home
  • Don't make excessive amounts for storage
  • Don't waste coffee or milk
  • Don't use the last of anything without reporting
  • Don't leave messes for others
  • Don't monopolize equipment during peak hours

How to Contribute

When supplies run low, here's how you can help keep the coffee flowing

Tip Jar

Drop spare change or small bills in the tip jar located next to the coffee machine. Every contribution helps!

Suggested: $1-5 per week if you're a regular user

Supply Purchases

Volunteer to purchase specific supplies when needed. Keep receipts for potential reimbursement from the tip jar.

Coordinate: Check with office admin before purchasing

Digital Payments

Some offices may set up digital payment methods like Venmo or office expense accounts for easier contributions.

Check: Ask office admin about available options

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our quota and contribution system

What happens if I can't contribute financially?

No problem! Financial contributions are completely voluntary. You can contribute in other ways like helping with cleaning, maintenance, or simply being considerate with usage. Everyone is welcome to enjoy the coffee corner.

How much should I contribute?

There's no set amount - contribute what feels comfortable for you. Many regular users contribute $1-5 per week, but any amount helps. Consider how often you use the coffee corner and what you might spend on coffee elsewhere.

Who manages the tip jar money?

Typically, the office administrator or a designated volunteer manages the tip jar funds. They use the money to purchase supplies when quotas are exceeded and keep receipts for transparency.

What if supplies run out completely?

If critical supplies run out and there aren't enough contributions to restock, the coffee corner may temporarily close until the next monthly allocation or until enough funds are available. This is why contributions are so important!

Can I bring my own coffee beans?

While the focus is on shared supplies, bringing your own special beans occasionally is usually fine. Just be sure to clean the grinder afterward and don't mix different beans in the hopper.
